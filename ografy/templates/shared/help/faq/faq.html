Frequently Asked Questions

What does Ografy do?

Ografy collects information from your online accounts and makes it easy to search everything you have done from one place.

Is my data secure?

Ografy is committed to keeping your data secure. Data searches have tight restrictions to make sure that only verified users can access data, and they can only access their own data.

Are you going to sell my data?

Ografy does not sell your data to anyone else. Our income comes from paid premium accounts and developer licenses.

Why can't I re-estimate locations more often?

Re-estimating the locations of Events is computationally expensive; it requires iterating through each one of your Events, finding the ones that are estimated, then for each of those finding one or two locations closest to it in time so that an estimation can be made. With each user having hundreds or thousands of Events, allowing everyone to re-estimate as much as they want would become taxing on our servers.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings -> Account, then click the "Delete Account" button. You will be prompted to confirm this action.
