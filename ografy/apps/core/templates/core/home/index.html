Looks like you're new

No worries. Welcome to Ografy!

Let's get you up to speed.

Create an Account

You can sign up with the info you entered up top, or you can change it now.

You can also sign up with an OpenID.

We'll create an account for you with us and maintain some basic information.

Edit Basic Settings

If you'd like to fill out your profile a bit more you can now enter some basic info.

None of this info is required, but it help to share with friends.

By default all of your personal information is hidden in your public profile.

You're free to decide what to show.

These settings can always be changed and will update immediately.

Connect Your Accounts

You can authorize Ografy to view your other accounts and collect data on your behalf.

You can start collecting your data from each connected account immediately.

You'll have to add each connected account separately.

We'll let you know exactly what information will be sent and received.

The accounts we support are listed explicitly here.

We'll be sure to let you know if we add, modify, or remove support for any of these services in the future.

Select Your Data

You can configure each account and specify the data that will be retrieved.

Ografy will quantify and categorize the information it gathers and suggest formats.

You can override the assumptions Ografy makes about certain data types

Publish Yourself

You can group gathered data and visualize it with the Ografy visualization engine.

You can annotate visualizations, adjust color schemes, and import graphic assets to create custom infographics.

You can share these infographics on your personal website, blog, or social network feeds.

Sit Back, Relax!

Continue spending time with the accounts you already use!

Ografy will continue to collect data from your connected accounts.

You can disconnect an account at any time.

Simply login and update your connection settings.

We'll be sure to delete any residual data.

How It Works

Ografy collects the information you generate.

Your data is re-streamed in a single place.

You can automatically track your activity and easily share cool stats with your friends and the world.

You can easily manage how your data is tracked and published.

What's Next?

Ografy was designed to minimize the learning curve.

However, more advanced features are available for users who'd like to spend more time.

If you'd like more detailed control over how your data is visualized, be sure to check out the documentation.

Ografy is continually updated with new features.

If you'd like to check in on the team's status and the planned features, be sure to check out the development blog.
