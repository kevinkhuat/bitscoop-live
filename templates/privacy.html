I.               INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We collect “Non-Personal Information” and “Personal Information.”  Non-Personal Information includes information that cannot be used to personally identify you, such as anonymous usage data, ip addresses, general demographic information we may collect, referring/exit pages and URLs, platform types, meta and geo data from your content, preferences you submit and preferences that are generated based on the data you submit and number of clicks.  Personal Information includes your username, email, and password, and other personal information which you submit or provide us access to when registering for and using our site. If you register with us through a Third-Party platform or service (e.g. facebook, twitter), we may access the information related to your Third-Party profile.

1.        Information collected via Technology

To access our homepage you do not need to submit any Personal Information.  However, in an effort to improve the quality of the Service, we track information provided to us by your browser, our browser plugins, or by our software application when you view or use the Service, such as the website you came from (known as the “referring URL”), the type of browser you use, the device from which you connected to the Service, the time and date of access, your location, your internet or browser history, IP address, and other information that does not personally identify you.  We track this information using cookies, tracking code, small text files which include an anonymous unique identifier, or other technology.  Cookies are sent to a user’s browser from our servers and are stored on the user’s computer hard drive.  Sending a cookie to a user’s browser enables us to collect Non-Personal information about that user, and in conjunction with Personal information when associated with an account or a logged in user, and keep a record of the user’s preferences when utilizing our services, both on an individual and aggregate basis.  The Company may use both persistent and session cookies and cache; persistent cookies and cache remain on your device after you close your session and until you delete them, while session cookies and cache expire when you close your browser. Some information from these cookies and cache may be synced to your cloud based services over which we have no control.  

Some Third-Party analytics, advertising or other services on our site may also place their own cookies or anonymous identifiers on your browser. For example, Google Analytics, Google Display Network, Google Analytics Demographics and Interest Reporting may be implemented whereby Google will set cookies or place tracking code to collect your data. You may opt-out of providing the data associated with these Google services by using their browser plugin found at: https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout/

In addition to the information provided automatically by your browser when you visit the Site, to become a subscriber to the Service you will need to create a personal profile.  You can create a profile by registering with the Service and entering your email address, and creating a user name and a password.  By registering, you are authorizing us to collect, store and use your email address in accordance with this Privacy Policy.
